Description of triangles

A triangle is a polygon with three edges and three vertices.It is one of the basic shapes in geometry

The types of triangles include;

  1. Equilateral triangle - is a triangle in which all three sides are equal
  2. Isosceles triangle - is a triangle that has two sides of equal lengt
  3. Scalene triangle - is a triangle that has three unequal sides
